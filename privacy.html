Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to HelpStash. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application ("App").

Key Point: HelpStash is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data, and everything is stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Timer Data: Purchase amounts, categories, duration preferences, and timer outcomes (resisted/purchased)
  • User Statistics: Streak count, money saved totals, timer completion history
  • Settings: Notification preferences, haptic feedback settings, custom timer presets

2.2 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Device identifiers
  • ❌ Browsing history or external app usage
  • ❌ Contacts or photos

3. How We Store Your Data

3.1 Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers.

3.2 iCloud Sync

Current Status: iCloud sync is NOT enabled. Your data remains exclusively on your device.

If iCloud sync is added in the future, you will be notified and given the option to opt in. iCloud data is encrypted and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Subscription & Payment Information

HelpStash uses Apple's StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases. When you subscribe:

  • Apple processes your payment information (we never see your credit card)
  • Apple provides us with a receipt to verify your subscription status
  • We do not store or have access to your payment details

Your payment information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Analytics & Usage Data

We do NOT collect any analytics or usage data. HelpStash is a local-only app - all your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

This means:

  • ❌ No tracking of feature usage
  • ❌ No crash reports sent to us
  • ❌ No behavioral analytics
  • ❌ No third-party analytics services (no Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)

Why? Your financial behavior is private. We designed HelpStash to never require an internet connection or server infrastructure.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We cannot share your data because we do not collect it. Since HelpStash stores all data locally on your device (not on our servers), we have no data to sell, rent, share, or disclose.

Legal Requests: If law enforcement requests user data, we cannot provide it because we don't have it. Any legal process would need to be directed at you and your device, not us.

Apple Subscription Data: The only information we have access to is your subscription status (via Apple's receipt verification). This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, not ours.

7. Your Rights & Data Control

7.1 Access Your Data

All your data is visible within the app's Stats and Settings screens. You have complete control over your information.

7.2 Delete Your Data

To delete all your data:

  1. Open the app → Settings
  2. Scroll to "Delete All Data"
  3. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data.

7.3 Export Data

Currently, data export is not available. You can manually review all your data within the Stats screen.

8. Children's Privacy

HelpStash is not directed to children under 13, and we do not collect personal information from users.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Data stored locally on your device (protected by iOS security)
  • Encrypted communication with Apple services (StoreKit)
  • No transmission of personal data to external servers

Note: No method of storage is 100% secure. Protect your device with a passcode or biometric authentication.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Update to "Last Updated" date at the top of this page

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under GDPR. However, because HelpStash stores all data locally on your device (not on our servers), most GDPR data requests do not apply to us:

  • Right to Access: We have no data about you to provide. All your data (purchase history, stats, streaks) is stored locally on your device. You can view it anytime in the app's Stats screen.
  • Right to Rectification: You can edit your data directly in the app (e.g., edit purchase amounts, delete individual entries). We do not store your data, so there is nothing for us to correct.
  • Right to Erasure: We have no data to delete. To delete your local data, see Section 7.2 (delete from app Settings → Data & Privacy, or uninstall the app).
  • Right to Data Portability: We cannot export your data because we do not collect or store it. Your data never leaves your device.

Apple Subscription Data: The only information related to you that we can access is your subscription status (via Apple's receipt verification). This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. To exercise GDPR rights regarding your Apple ID or subscription data, contact Apple directly.

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@helpstash.app. Please note we cannot fulfill data access/deletion requests for data we do not have.

12. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Here's how HelpStash complies:

  • Right to Know: We do not collect or store any personal information about you. All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Right to Delete: We have no data to delete. To delete your local data, see Section 7.2 (delete from app Settings → Data & Privacy, or uninstall the app).
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information. We never have, and never will.
  • Non-Discrimination: We do not discriminate based on exercise of CCPA rights.

Apple Subscription Data: For CCPA requests regarding your Apple ID or subscription data, contact Apple directly.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us:

  • Email: support@helpstash.app
  • Website: helpstash.app
  • Support Page: helpstash.app/support

Response Time: We typically respond within 48 hours.
